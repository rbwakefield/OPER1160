Introduction to Psychology

Task-based Language Teaching is a more inductive form of the Communicative Approach, made famous by N. Prabhu who implemented Task-based Language Teaching in schools in India in the 1970’s and 80’s – an experiment which he called the Bangalore Project. Dave and Jane Willis are also famous proponents of Task-based Language Teaching. They have written and presented extensively on the subject. Since the method has become influential across the globe, including in Canada, it is important to understand it.
