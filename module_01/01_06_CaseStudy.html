Introduction to Operations Management (OM)

Case Study:

Hard Rock Café Illustrating some of the 10 Operations Decision Areas

Let’s stop and think about a restaurant you might have been to: Hard Rock Café. The following video shows it is much more than a restaurant—they provide an experience. They provide quality food in an entertaining environment but also note they have a retail store. Did you know that their retail sales (shirts, etc.) account for nearly the same amount of revenue as their awesome food? This video points out some of the 10 operations management decisions that you’ll see in this text and that we’ll cover in this course. It also serves to illustrate that operations management is just as important in services as in manufacturing.
