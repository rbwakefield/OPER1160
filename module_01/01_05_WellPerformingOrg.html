Introduction to Operations Management (OM)

Functions of a Well-Performing Organization

To create goods and services, all organizations perform three functions:

Marketing

Generates the demand and takes the order for the product or service.

Finance or Accounting

Tracks how the organization is doing in monetary terms, pays the bills and collects the monies.

Operations

Creates the product or service.
