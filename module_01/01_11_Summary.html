Introduction to Operations Management (OM)

Summary and References

With a basic understanding of Supply Chain Management, why we study it, some of the trends in the industry, what OM managers do, and some of the associations associated with this field and certifications, we are now ready to move on to look at the history. In addition, we’ll look at one of the most basic and important calculations – productivity plus, discuss the differences in goods and services.
