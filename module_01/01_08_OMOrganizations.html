Introduction to Operations Management (OM)

OM Organizations Worth Checking Out

If you want to build on your degree or diploma with specific certifications, network with industry professionals or learn more about this growing and important field, here are some organizations you should be checking out:

APICS (Association for Operations Management)

Provides research, education and certifications for CPIM, CSCP or CLTD.

Website

Canadian Supply Chain Sector Council (CSCSC)

work with all members involved in the Supply Chain fields to identify and meet the industry’s HR needs.

Website

Council of Supply Chain Management Professionals (CSCMP)

provides education, tools and networking opportunities for its members.

Website

Excellence Canada (formerly NQI)

A non-profit that helps Canadian companies improve their quality. They also issue the Canada Awards for Excellence.

Website

Institute for Supply Management (ISM)

Concentrates on research, issuing an industry magazine, education and certification.

Website

Project Management Institute (PMP)

Supports the PM profession and issues certifications, standards research and provides networking opportunities.

Website

Standards Council of Canada (SCC)

Canada’s national accreditation group for standards and conformity.

Website

Supply Chain Management Association

Sets standards and provide professional development and accreditation in supply chain management in Canada.
