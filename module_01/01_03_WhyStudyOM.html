Introduction to Operations Management (OM)

Why Do We Study OM?

We know that OM is an important part of any business, so we study OM for four different reasons (Lopers et al., 2016, p.7):

1

To see how people organize themselves for
productive enterprise.

Since it is one of the three major functions of any organization, it is important to know how it works in relationship to the other business areas.

2

To know how goods and services are produced.

Would you like to know how the goods and services you use daily are created?

3

To know what Operations Managers do in any
type of organization.

By understanding this you become more valuable to your current or future employer.

4

Because it is such a costly part of an organization.

Think about the factory or offices, inventory, people and processes happening everyday
