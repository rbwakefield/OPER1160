Introduce Yourself (Non-Graded)

Take a moment to introduce yourself including what program you are taking by posting on the Week 1 Discussion Board “Introduce Yourself” .

Provide a little background information regarding your past experience in the Operations Management field from jobs, or volunteer positions. Remember Supply Chain and OM is far-reaching and NOT just manufacturing or production!

Some Tips

  • Do not use “text-speak” (e.g. "u r" or "i" or "lol" etc).
  • Please use full sentences and carefully proof your work for grammar and spelling.
  • A hint would be to complete it in a Word document then cut and paste it into the Discussion Posting .

Be sure to check out information on how to use the discussion board.
