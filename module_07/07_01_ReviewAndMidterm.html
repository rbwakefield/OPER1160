Review and Midterm

Week 7 – Review and Midterm

You will write the midterm exam in week 7. It will be out of 100 marks and worth 25% of the semester mark.

Midterm Overview

The test is 2 hours long and will cover the first 4 unit outcomes on the course outline (Chapters 1-4). The midterm will be a mixture of multiple choice questions, analysis, short answers and definitions.

How to Prepare for the Midterm

  1. Read the course outline and look at the individual unit outcomes.
  2. Review the weekly units, provided exercises, your study notes and your completed assignments.
  3. Review any calculation questions you have done.
  4. Use the midterm reviews sample questions that are have provided.
  5. After you have completed the above, review the course outline unit outcomes and see if you can answer each one. If you can, you are prepared.
  6. Be aware of your schools exam writing policies.

Midterm Exam Formulae to Note

This Guidance Document has most of the key formulae studied prior to the midterm. Please be aware that you may need to use formulae that are not on this document to solve some questions. Therefore, ensure that you are aware of all formulae and methodologies studied during the term.

For Mid-Term Exam

  • Productivity = Output/Input
  • Multifactor Productivity = Output/(Sum of Inputs)
    • Do not have to calculate but note some of the common factor for inputs.
  • % Change = (New-Old) / Old * 100% Note: Indicate increase or decrease
  • Cycle Time = total elapsed time to complete a process once
  • % Value Added Time = (value added time / cycle time) * 100%
