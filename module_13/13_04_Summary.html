Business Planning Process

Summary and References

The Business Planning Process is important to coordinate the functional areas to produce the product/service to satisfy the customer. First, the overall strategic planning sets the course. Then the tactical planning (S&OP, MPS, MRP) is done. Then, it gets done in the execution and control phase.

In our next unit we will delve into the tactical planning areas in more detail. It is important to examine what is needed but also the capacities to ensure we can meet our customer needs.
