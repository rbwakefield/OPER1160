Operations Strategy in a Global Environment

Summary and References

With technological advances, changing political views and improved distribution channels, going global is a huge part of today’s operations. This unit has looked at six reasons companies choose to expand globally. Then, paused to explain the importance of mission statements and strategies and the three different competitive strategies companies use (differentiation, low cost value, responsiveness) combined with their Key Success Factors and their own company’s Core Competencies to succeed here and abroad. Finally, realizing that companies expand across the globe in different ways we examined the four international operations strategies they use to be successful!

Next up we look at developing products and services. We examine four reasons companies develop new products and services and six dimensions operations focus on as they develop them. Then we look at the process and various elements involved in bringing out these new products.
