Operations and Productivity

Discussion: Ethics and CSR in Operations

Overview

This discussion is to encourage you to become aware of current ethical and CSR considerations in the field of operations.

This discussion will be completed the Ethics and CSR Discussion Board. Refer to the Discussion 1 rubric for more details.

Week Due: Week 2 – see the Instructional Plan.
Value of Assignment: 1%

Purpose

To learn more about how the 10 Critical Decision areas of Operations Management are used in a goods producing or service company or your choice. This knowledge will help you understand how Operations Management supports corporate strategies.

Instructions

In the Discussion Board Ethics and CSR in Operations :

  1. Post a link to an article link from current sources (within last 6 months) that illustrates an operational ethical or CSR challenge that a company has faced.
  2. Then, in your own words, in one or short paragraphs, comment on how the article illustrated the ethnical challenge and tie it back to this week’s lesson.

Be sure to check out information on how to use the discussion board.
