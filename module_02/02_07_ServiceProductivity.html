Service Productivity – Harder to Improve

The Challenges of Improving Services

Measuring service productivity and therefore increasing productivity can be challenging. Let’s consider five reasons for this;

  1. Many services are labour intensive… helping more customers doesn’t necessarily mean it would be better….customers may complain if rushed
  2. Frequently focused on unique individual attributes or desires… for example, food may be sent back if it doesn’t meet the diner’s expectations
  3. Services are often performed by professionals and productivity may differ by how well are they trained or educated… we all want a well-trained medical professional who doesn’t have to look up information
  4. Services may be hard to automate and put through in quantity… service providers need breaks and can’t run non-stop 24/7.
  5. It is often hard to evaluate for quality… performance of a teacher or professor

Although there are difficulties in improving productivity in the service sectors, improvements are being made. Indeed, when management focuses attention on how work actually gets done the results can be astonishing. (Lopers et al., 2016, p. 20)

Textbook Reading

Read “OM in Action: Taco Bell improves productivity and goes green to lower costs”, in your textbook (Lopers et al., 2016, p. 20). Now, consider some of the actions taken by Taco Bell to increase productivity that have resulted in Taco Bell’s ability to serve “twice the volume with half the labour”. When you have some ideas about these actions, take a look below to read more about them.

Reveal Taco Bell's Productivity Actions
  • Taco Bell designed meals that were easy to prepare
  • Actual cooking and food preparation was done elsewhere
  • Automation and an efficient layout were introduced to save preparation time and time to serve customers at the drive-through window
  • Empowerment of their employees to make more operational decisions combined with better training for their managers meant they could manage more restaurants and thus reduce the headcount used just for management
  • From a multi-factor productivity and cost savings angle they also had savings by installing water and energy saving grills
