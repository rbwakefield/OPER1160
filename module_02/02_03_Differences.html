Differences Between Goods and Services

Manufacturers produce a tangible product, while service products are often intangible.

Now, think of the last restaurant meal you had. Do you remember just the food (product) or was it the wait staff (service) that made your meal memorable? Many products are a combination of a good and a service which complicates the definition. Tangible products are those that you can see and touch before purchasing

The distinction between goods and services is important for operations managers making decisions. As you examine this chart pick a product and service like cars (product) and getting a haircut (service).
