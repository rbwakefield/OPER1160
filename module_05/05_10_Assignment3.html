Overview

This assignment is based on content from Week 5 and Week 6. This assignment will give you hands-on experience with some of the more common and useful TQM tools. This assignment is due at the end of week 6 but it is important to start preparing now. The tools outlined in this assignment will be on the midterm exam.

This assignment will be completed and submitted to the Assignment 2 dropbox. Refer to the Assignment 2 rubric in for more details.

Week Due: End of Week 6 – see the Instructional Plan.
Value of Assignment: 3%

Instructions, Part One

Step 1:

Develop a process map for:

  1. An oil change at a car dealership OR
  2. A haircut/style

Step 2:

Use at least 3 basic symbols and approximately 10 steps in total. Each step in your process should have an average time included

Step 3:

You can refer to Week 6 instructions for the Basic Symbols for Process Mapping or Flowcharting and the example in the Process Map for Shopping.

Step 4:

Use the process map to calculate the cycle time and percent value added time (VAT) for your process drawing. VAT will be determined by you but must be “valuable” to the end customer.

Instructions, Part Two

Step 1:

Create a Cause and Effect or Fishbone Diagram using at least 4 M’s to help you find the root cause for:

  1. Why the oil change at a car dealership was inadequate, OR
  2. A customer was not happy with the haircut/style they just received

Step 2:

You must have at least 2 causes for each of the 4 M’s selected. Refer to Week 6 for more information.

Formatting Requirements for the Paper Submission

  • Used Calibri font size 12 for the body of the report.
  • Put a title on each page that includes your choice of company.
  • In the footer on each page, include your name and the page number.
  • No title page is required.
