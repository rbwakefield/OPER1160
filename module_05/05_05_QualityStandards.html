Quality Standards

Many different organizations provide quality standards often targeted to different industries or professions.

For instance, Accreditation Canada does this for health care and social service organizations.

International Organization for Standardization (ISO)

A common one you’ll see is the International Organization for Standardization (ISO). It is an independent, non-governmental international organization with over 165 countries represented. In Canada, the Standards Council of Canada (SCC, 2017), a crown corporation, is the ISO representative.

ISO is recognized world over as the international standards that support innovation and progress. ISO set standards to, as they put it, “make things work”. (iso.org, 2017) They set world-class specifications for products, services and systems, to ensure quality, safety, and efficiency which helps facilitates world-trade.
