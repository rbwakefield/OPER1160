Discussion: Quality

Overview:

This discussion will help you explore how quality influences many areas of our lives and all aspects of business. Refer to the Discussion 3 rubric for more details.

Week Due: End of Week 5 – see the Instructional Plan.
Value of Assignment: 1% as part of Best 2 of 3 for weeks 2, 4, and 5

Instructions:

In the Discussion Board Quality :

  • Find an article, advertisement from a newspaper or magazine, a picture from work or a picture you take that ties to the Quality unit. Examples include; a quality certification, a chart that tracks/measures quality, an example of a cost of quality, or a company using quality as part of their advertising.
  • In your own words, write a short description of what you’ve found and how it ties to a concept covered in Unit 4.
  • Provide the link or attach a picture and post your summary to the Discussion Board.
  • Include your source using APA.

Be sure to check out information on how to use the discussion board.
