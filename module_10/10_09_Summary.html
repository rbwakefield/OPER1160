Supply Management II

Summary and References

In the next unit, we will examine the logistics side of supply chain management. After looking at the strategic importance, we will look at the major decision areas: transportation modes, warehousing, logistics information systems, and material handling and packaging. With the thought “you can’t manage what you can’t measure” we’ll also look at two logistics’ measurements…the perfect order and landed costs. We’ll end that unit looking at reverse logistics systems where products are brought back into the supply chain.
