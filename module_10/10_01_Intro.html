Introduction

Supply Chain Management is continually evolving. New trends and best practices are being created and used every day by the supply chain leaders in their industries. Companies such as Wal-Mart in retail, Apple in personal electronics, Toyota in automotive, Exxon Mobil in oil & gas are using many of these trends and best practices in their business models, integrating their total supply chain so it runs as efficiently and effectively as possible. As many companies in the same industry have similar products or services, it is often their supply chain management practices that separate them from the competition. Most of the concepts that are examined here are not in your text.

The goal of supplier selection is to use a decision model to evaluate several suppliers and select the best one using objective numerical scores. In this second part, we will examine the weighted-point evaluation system as it is commonly used and easy to understand when compared to many others. This model is the basis for the preparation of a sourcing recommendation in the Group Project (20 % of your mark). In addition to the example in your text, there will be a couple others provided for practice.
