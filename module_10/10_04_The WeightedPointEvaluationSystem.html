Supply Management II

The Weighted-Point Evaluation System

This is a common decision model used to evaluate suppliers objectively.

It identifies key criteria and assigns numerical scores to performance. This type of decision model works best when there are several criteria and alternative suppliers to be considered, with no clear leader. A total score for each supplier will be calculated by assigning weights to our selected performance criteria and then rating the suppliers on these criteria.

Score = ∑ y = 1 n Performance XY × W Y Score = sum from{y=1} to{n} Performance rsub{XY} TIMES W rsub{Y}
where : X = supplier X Y = performance dimension Y alignl stack{where: # X = supplier X # Y = performance dimension Y}
Performance XY = rated performance of supplier X with regard to performance dimension Y . Performance_{XY} = rated performance of supplier X with regard to performance dimension Y.
W Y = assigned weight for performance dimension Y , where ∑ y = 1 n W Y = 1 W_{Y} = assigned weight for performance dimension Y, where sum from{y=1} to{n} W_{Y} = 1
