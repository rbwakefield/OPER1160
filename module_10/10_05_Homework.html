What are the purposes of a Supply Chain Management Program?
  • Procurement of materials and services
  • Transform them into intermediate goods and services
  • Deliver these products through a distribution channel
Why did they elect to leave the large supply group and form a new purchasing group?
  • Previously they were with a national buying group that dropped and/or changed some of the approved and preferred suppliers
  • The new, local group was able to reduce cost by using more creative sourcing. They gained better control of prices, quality, and delivery

After 1 year, what was the amount saved and from where?

  • Also, standardization (example of pace makers)
  • Long-term relationships with the suppliers

C) Based on the results, should Flynn Industries single source or not? What might stop Flynn from single-sourcing? Check your answer.

If Carrier B has the capacity to handle all of Flynn's business, then Flynn might consider single-sourcing with carrier B and try to work out a better price. On the other hand, a multiple or dual sourcing arrangement would provide Flynn with an alternative provider if Carrier B runs into difficulties.
