Logistics

Summary and References

In this unit, we examined why logistics is critical to an organization. Transportation modes and warehousing solutions and logistics decision areas were examined. Then, we stressed how logistics strategies need to align with and support overall business strategies.

Our topic ties directly with logistics as we will look at inventory. We will look at the various reasons we utilize inventory and different types of inventory. We’ll look at independent and dependent demand and two review systems – periodic and continuous. Then learn to calculate important inventory metrics such as reorder point, and the “best” order quantity (Economic Order Quantity – EOQ).
