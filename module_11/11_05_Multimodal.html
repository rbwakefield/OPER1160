Multimodal or Containerization

To facilitate seamless, integrated transportation from the origin to the destination, companies often use more than one mode of transportation so they turn to multimodal solutions.

Using standardized containers that can be easily and quickly packed at a source then moved between trucks, flatcars of trains, and ships, without unloading and reloading of materials, to reach the ultimate destination, a company can realize significant time and cost savings. Below are the images of two trucks. One is multimodal. Can you tell which one?

Light, expensive materials may even be packed in various shapes of containers designed to fit perfectly in the holds of aircraft then shifted to trucks for quick, efficient delivery to end customers.
