Introduction

As supply chain management becomes more critical, businesses are more actively managing logistics in an effort to improve their cost, flexibility, and delivery performance. The purpose of this unit is to introduce you to the field of logistics. We will look at its’ strategic importance, the choice of transportation modes, warehousing, logistics information systems and performance measures. Logistics will continue to grow in scope and importance with the increase in global trade and the associated information flows.

But first, let’s check out this “view” of logistics .
