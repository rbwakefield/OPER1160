WEEK 1
 

ASSOCIATED COURSE LEARNING OUTCOME(S)
  •  Apply professional career management tools, techniques, and strategies, using current technologies, to position oneself as an ideal candidate with targeted employers.

  • Evaluate personal and professional growth through reflective practice to make informed decisions regarding career directions.
