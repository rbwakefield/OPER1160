Welcome to the Course

Hello, and welcome to TESL 006: The Structure and Sound of English II. In this sixth course of the TESL Certificate program, you will build on the foundations of The Structure and Sound of English I. You will study complex grammatical structures and pronunciation features of the English language and identify strategies for overcoming first language interference in learning English as an additional language. You will integrate a complex framework for the effective teaching and assessment of grammar and pronunciation into the lesson planning process.

The first module in each of the 8 courses in this program serves as our equivalent of the first day of the course.  It is important in an ESL class to get to know your learners so that you can plan for their learning. It is also important for your learners to get to know each other so that they can feel comfortable learning and practising language together. We encourage you to engage in these opening tasks so that you become acquainted with the fellow students together with whom you will be learning how to teach!
