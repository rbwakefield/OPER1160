Group Work

Beginning with week 1, you will be randomly assigned to a group of your peers.

Throughout the course, you will work with your group in a number of ways:

  • You will work with your group on weekly in-class exercises and will be asked to provide feedback regarding your groupâ€™s worksheets as prompted by the instructor.
  • You will discuss important ideas and topics with group members in weekly Group Discussion Forums.
  • Your group will also be your audience for your weekly blog posts (you will have to assign access to each member).

Beginning with week 10, you will begin be working with your group on an ongoing case study (See: Group Project Case Study). You will work together to resolve the scenario between weeks 10 and 15, and collaborate to deliver a group presentation on the final week. You will complete a self- and group-assessment at the end of the course.
