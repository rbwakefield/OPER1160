Course Information

Discussion Topics

The following sections contain the questions to guide your weekly group discussions in TESL 006 - The Structure and Sound of English II. Go to Guidelines for Online Discussion Boards to read more about the expectations and directions for using discussion boards on eConestoga. The discussion boards give students the opportunity to demonstrate that they have read and critically reflected on the material for discussion. It also allows students to challenge themselves and others to investigate and evaluate the material.

There is an overall discussion topic every week as an opportunity for you to post questions and comments.

In addition, each week contains activities to help you learn and reflect on the course material, some of which are tied to discussion topics.

At the end of each Week, a Preparation Task will provide you with the opportunity to put what you are learning into practice in preparation for the actual assessments. This is also often tied to a discussion topic.
