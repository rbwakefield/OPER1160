Quizzes

These assessments are designed to test students’ comprehension of the key concepts, terms, and themes of Weeks 3, 4, 5, 6, 7 and 8.

Week 4 Quiz

Instructions: Complete all 25 multiple-choice questions. The Week 4 Quiz will be administered via eConestoga. Correct questions will be assigned one point each, to a total of 20 points. It is worth 2.5% of the 5% total weight for all quizzes.

Weeks 3, 5, 6, 7 and 8 Quizzes

These quizzes will be delivered in-class by the instructor. Each quiz is worth 10% of the 5% total weight for all quizzes.

Value: 5% final grade.
