Product and Service Design

Discussion: Example of a New Product

Overview:

This discussion will help you explore new products and related it to this week’s learning. This discussion will use the Example of a New Product Discussion Board. Refer to the Discussion 2 rubric for more details.

Week Due: End of Week 4 – see the Instructional Plan.
Value of Assignment: 1%

Instructions:

In the Discussion Board Example of a New Product :

  • Post a link to an article from current sources (within the last 2 years) that illustrates a new product or service.
  • In your own words, write a short paragraph or two and comment on what the company had to think about as they brought out this new product
  • Tie your comments to the material covered in this week
  • Provide the link or picture for us to read and cite it using APA

Be sure to check out information on how to use the discussion board.
