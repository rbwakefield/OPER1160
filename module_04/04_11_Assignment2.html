Assignment 2: Critical Decisions of OM Management

Overview

In this assignment you will complete Company research exploring the OM Decision Areas in the chosen company. You are to use the same company chosen for Assignment 1: Career Research. This assignment is due at the end of week 4. Please see the Evaluations section and the Instructional Plan for more details. If is recommended that you review the details of this assignment now and begin to prepare for completion.

This assignment will be completed and submitted to the Assignment 2 dropbox. Refer to the Assignment 2 rubric in for more details.

Week Due: End of Week 4 – see the Instructional Plan.
Value of Assignment: 3%

Purpose

To learn more about how the 10 Critical Decision areas of Operations Management are used in a goods producing or service company or your choice. This knowledge will help you understand how Operations Management supports corporate strategies.

Instructions

Step 1:

Research how the company you selected for Assignment # 1, uses any six of the ten Critical Decisions of Operations Management. These OM Decisions are outlined in Table 1.2 or Table 2.2 in your text. Answering the questions in Table 1.2 may be useful in starting your research. You may find corporate websites and/or business databases provided by your College useful. If an information shortage is encountered, contact your instructor for permission to change companies.

Step 2:

Summarize your research for each of the selected six decision areas in one or two paragraphs. You must provide content that is unique to your company, not just common to the industry segment they represent. Your instructor wants to learn about the company you have chosen.

Formatting Requirements for the Paper Submission

  • Used Calibri font size 12 for the body of the report.
  • Put a title on each page that includes your choice of company.
  • In the footer on each page, include your name and the page number.
  • No title page is required.
