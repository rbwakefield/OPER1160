Product and Service Design

Summary and References

In this unit, we looked at product and service design and process development. We looked at WHY companies develop new products and services plus WHAT they need to look at internally. Then, we moved onto the five stages of the process development and some of the roles that the various functional areas will take on as they roll out new products or services. We finished up with some basic concepts that are important to understand including sequential and concurrent processes, black and grey box design, standardization and modularity, value analysis and target costing.

In the next unit, we’ll explore another of the Operations Management decision areas – Quality.
