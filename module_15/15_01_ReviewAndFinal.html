Review and Final Exam

Week 15 – Review and Final Exam

You will write the Final Exam in week 15. It will be out of 100 marks and worth 25% of the semester mark.

Final Exam Overview

The test is 2 hours long and will cover the last 4 Unit Outcomes on the course outline (Units 5 – 8; Chapters 5 – 7 plus Custom Chapter). The final exam consists of a mixture of multiple choice, analysis, short answer and definitions.

How to Prepare for the Midterm

  1. Read the course outline and look at the individual unit outcomes.
  2. Review the weekly units, provided exercises, the notes you made and the assignments you completed.
  3. Review any calculation questions you have done and use the Final Exam reviews that are provided.
  4. At the end of your study review the unit outcomes and see if you can answer each one. If you can, you are prepared.
  5. After you have completed the above, review the course outline unit outcomes and see if you can answer each one. If you can, you are prepared.
  6. Be aware of your school's exam writing policies.

Final Exam Formulae to Note

This Guidance Document has most of the key formulae studied between the Mid-Term and Final. Please be aware that you may need to use formulae that are not on this document to solve some questions. Therefore, ensure that you are aware of all formulae and methodologies studied during the term.

For Final Exam

  • Total Holding and Ordering Costs per Year = (Order Quantity/2) * Annual Holding Cost per Unit + (Annual Demand/Order Quantity) * Ordering Cost
  • Economic Order Quantity = square root of (2 * Yearly Demand * Setup Cost/Holding Cost)
  • Re-Order Point = Daily Demand * Lead Time + Safety Stock
  • Inventory Turns = Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) per Year/Inventory Investment
  • Total Weighted Point Average Score = Sum of (Weight * Performance Score) Note: Weights must = 1
  • Weeks of Supply = Inventory investment / Average Weekly COGS
  • Average Inventory = (Order Quantity / 2) + SS
