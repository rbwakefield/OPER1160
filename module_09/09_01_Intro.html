Introduction

How do the products and services you use every day get to you? Where did they come from? Every product or service comes to us through a supply chain. In fact, your college diploma is brought to you through a supply chain and you are an active participant.

This chapter on supply management explores this very broad topic. You will be introduced to the activities and challenges that make up supply management (sourcing or purchasing) which should give you an appreciation for why it is critical to the success of many companies. Your team case (introduced in the next unit) will rely heavily on this chapter. We will follow a Strategic Sourcing Process Model then the Procure-to-Pay (P2P) cycle. We will discover how sourcing strategies impact “make vs. buy” (insourcing vs. outsourcing) decisions and finish with a look at trends in supply management.
