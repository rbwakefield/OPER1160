Profit Leverage Practice Questions

These practice questions are not for marks.

Example One

A) If a company has cost savings in its COGS of $1,500 how much will it increase their pretax profits?

Check Your Answer

It is $1 for $1 so pretax profits will increase by $1,500

B) Assume they make a pretax profit margin of 10% and they sell $1,500 more, how much more profit will they make?

Check Your Answer

$1,500 x 10% = $150

If they sell $1,500 with a pretax profit of 10% (10 cents for every $1 of sales) they will make only $150 additional pretax profits

C) Assuming they still make a pretax profit margin of 10%, to make a pretax profit of $1,500, how much more will they have to sell?

Check Your Answer

$1,500/.10 = $15,000

To make the same increase in pretax profits ($1,500) they would have to sell an additional $15,000

Example Two

Check Your Answer

$1 saved is a $1 earned… $50,000
