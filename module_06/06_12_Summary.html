Quality Management Tools

Summary and References

This chapter looked at quality tools business process measurements and that are important for TQM and operating a successful business. We covered producing a Check Sheet to collect data, a Fish-bone or Cause-and-Effect Diagram to organize date, a Pareto Chart to tell us what causes to focus on, and how to draw a Process Map to better understand the steps in a process.

This is the end of the first half of OPER1160. The midterm is next and covers all the units up until now.

After the midterm, we move into studying Supply Management which is also the basis for our team case.
