Business Process Measurements

Here are some business process measurements we are going to use for this unit.

They are used to monitor some activities of a company.

Productivity:
Remember the calculation for Single Factor Productivity?
Output / Inputs where inputs are person-hours of time.
Efficiency:
To measure or benchmark your productivity you measure against the standard. This might be against historical results or an industry process or a competitive benchmark. Calculate as (Actual productivity / Standard productivity) x 100 %
Cycle Time:
Time required to complete a process. Calculate as Elapsed Time/# of cycles.
% Value Added Time
(Effectiveness):
It is important to look at the amount of time spent on value added activities versus the total cycle time. Calculate as (Value Added Time / Total Cycle Time ) x 100%
